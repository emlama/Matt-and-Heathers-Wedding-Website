Orientation to the Area

While the wedding itself will be in a neighborhood called Charles Village, it is recommended that guests stay in Harbor East or close by at the Sleep Inn. To help orient you, there are four main neighborhoods to consider:

1) Harbor East where the Marriott is located, just east of the inner harbor, is home to a variety of new boutiques and stores (Anthropology and Urban Chic) as well as Whole Foods, a movie theatre and a variety of chain and local restaurants. It is a great area to walk around.

2) Inner Harbor is just next to Harbor East and was developed decades ago, but today is still the main tourist area with an aquarium and science museum.

3) Fells Point is home for us, located just east of Harbor East where cobblestone streets are lined with restaurants, bars, stores and more bars. This area has something for everyone including history and good music.

4) Little Italy is just north of Harbor East, a few blocks north of Whole Foods. It is a tiny area with a variety of restaurants - some good and some ok, but cute to walk around.

To Do

1) Baltimore Aquarium - a short walk away in the inner harbor, excellent exhibits and shark tank

2) Visionary Arts Museum - eclectic mix of art exhibits west of the inner harbor

3) Cat's Eye Pub - a personal favorite, great dive for live blues and rock and roll right in fells point

To Eat

1) Thames Street Oyster House - good for seafood and oysters in fells point, try the lobster salad

2) Bertha's Mussels - in fells point, best mussels in town

3) Stuggy's - Hot Dog take-out in fells point, try the crab mac and cheese.

4) Lebanese Taverna - good affordable lebanese food in harbor east

5) Cinghiale - upscale italian in harbor east if you are looking for something delightful

6) Ouzo Bay - new greek upscale place in harbor east with a good 5-7 happy hour

7) Peter's Inn - small bistro in fells point, a personal favorite with a menu that changes weekly (no reservations)

8) Pitango - owned by a neighbor, this fells point shop has awesome sorbet and gelato with flavors like raspberry, mojito, grapefruit and whatever is in season

9) Bonapart - for french pastries, and outdoor seating in fells point, great spot

10) Tortillaria Sinaloa - cheap take-out taco place, in fells point a few blocks from our house

To Drink

1) Wit and Wisdom - an upscale restaurant in the 4 Seasons, it has an area with sofas perfect for hanging out and catching up with a cocktail.

2) Lamil - for excellent coffee, this is located in the 4 Seasons in Harbor East

3) Vino - outside seating in fells point, good wine bar owned by locals
