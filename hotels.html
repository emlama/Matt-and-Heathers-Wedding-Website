Travel for Out-of-Towners

If you plan on staying at the Marriott, many things will be in walking distance and if you stay at the Sleep Inn, everything will be a cab ride away. We will provide transportation to and from the wedding for out-of-town guests. Pick up locations will be at the Sleep Inn and the Marriott. In your RSVP please indicate if you will need a shuttle to the wedding; more details will come later.

Accommodations

Courtyard Marriott

1000 Aliceanna Street, Baltimore, MD

Get driving directions

Rate
$169 a night plus 15% tax. The cutoff date for this rate is 3/22/2013.
However we suggest that you book as soon as possible in the case that we need to block-off more rooms.

Reservations
To reserve a room at the Courtyard Marriott you can do either of the following.

1. Call the hotel directly at 443-923-4000 and request the "Forr-Sarkissian Wedding Group Rate".

2. Go online to marriott.com/bwidt and click "Special Rates & Awards" on the left side. Use the group code "FSWFSWA" to reserve rooms with 1 king sized bed, or "FSWFSWB" to reserve rooms with 2 double beds.

Amenities
- Access to the MAC Gym (our excellent gym)
- An indoor swimming pool
- There is a parking fee of $23.

Location
Right in the Harbor East, everything will be in walking distance with no need for a car.
